Goodtags is a front end to the barbershoptags.com tag database with a focus on ease of use.

Key Features:

- Navigate seach results from within the sheet music view, to quickly find the right version of the tag you're looking for
- Retrieve tags by id number 
- Press a button in the sheet music to hear the root of the key the tag is in
- Listen to learning tracks while looking at sheet music
- Add favorites and organize them using labels
- Backup and sync your favorites across devices
- Pitch pipe
